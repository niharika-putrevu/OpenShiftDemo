Prospect Mentoring

Prospect provides the tools, guidance, and support high school seniors need during the entire college application process. We pair high school students virtually with current undergraduates based on the specific resources mentees need, giving specialized, accessible, long-term resources to each student.

POTENTIAL MAJOR & UNIVERSITIES

Once high school students are paired with their mentors, they can ask questions about a prospective major or specific university experiences, opportunities, and culture. If you prefer, we'll do our best to pair you with a student who attends your prospective university and can help you with supplements, interviews, and details about the school that can help you with your application.

APPLICATION PROCESS & ESSAYS

Having a mentor who has had recent knowledge and experience about the college application process by your side makes a big difference. Mentors will answer specific application related questions, for Common Application or Coalition, help brainstorm ideas for personal statements/short answers, provide constructive feedback, and keep you on track.

SCHOLARSHIPS AND RESOURCES

College is expensive. We're here to help you lighten your financial burden and give you all the resources you need. If you're planning to apply through QuestBridge and would like to learn more about the process, we will try our best to pair you with a QuestBridge scholar. You'd rather learn about research opportunities, scholarships, work-study, or financial aid? We've got you covered.
